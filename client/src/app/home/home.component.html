Rules

Hackathon Submission Video (Covers gameplay process and rules)
  1. Before the game begins, players bid in a random fashion, based on their cards. This bid number is the amount of points the player's partnership will have to collect in order to win.
  2. The highest bidder at the end of 30 seconds will be able to decide a 'trump' and 'partner' card. The trump card is the card a player can lay down to overthrow a higher rank card if they do not have the suit they need to put down during the playing section. The player with the partner card will be partners with the highest bidder.
  3. Next, the gameplay process begins, during which each player puts down a card with the same suit as the first card played. If they do not have the suit they can put down a card of the trump to overthrow the card for a higher value or put down a card of another suit for a lower vlaue.
  4. After each hand, the player with the highest card based on the trump suit and card ranks collects the entire hand, and gains a number of points equal to the number of points in the hand (5's = 5 points, 10's/Face Cards = 10 points, everything else = 0 points).
  5. This process repeats until all players run out of their cards, and if the partnership (highest bidder + player with partner card) have collected a number of points equal to or above the highest bid, they win, otherwise, they lose.

Tech Stack (Github)

  • Back End: Flask API (Python)
  • Database: mongoDB
  • Authentication: JWT
  • Realtime communication: SocketIO
